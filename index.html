Company

The AUDI AG stands for sporty vehicles, high build quality and progressive design – for “Vorsprung durch Technik.” The Audi Group is among the world’s leading producers of premium cars. To play an instrumental role in shaping the transformation as we head into a new age of mobility the Company is implementing its strategy step by step.Beauty – for Audi, that means one thing above all: progressive, attractive, fascinating. It's about inspiring people and at the same time being more than just superficial. Beauty can mean, for example, sporty forms that are full of character, or interactive materials – but also an intelligently solved problem, which you as an Audi customer will never even come to notice. Everything that Audi conceives of, from the new model to digital services, follows its claim of thoroughly thought-out, simple and thus perfect design.Audi stands for sporty vehicles, high build quality and progressive design – for “Vorsprung durch Technik.”The future begins today: with our visions, decisions and investments. At Audi, we are constantly searching for solutions that move people. First of all, that means understanding what’s important to them: today and tomorrow.

Careers

We are facing challenging times. That’s why we are constantly strengthening our team and becoming more agile. Work with us to develop new, sustainable mobility solutions for the future. Audi offers you flexibility, an innovative environment, freedom and internationality. We’re looking forward to hearing from you! Freedom for first professional experience: At eye level right from the start - after graduating from Audi: Internships, vocational training or one of our dual study programs - whatever path you take, you start directly in the Audi‘s world as an important part of the team, individually cared for front and center at all process. Free space for your ideas: Audi offers students an extensive set of opportunities to combine their theoretical knowledge with a practical experience while still pursuing their studies.
